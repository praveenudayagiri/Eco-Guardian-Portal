Published on May 2, 2024 Author: Jane Smith

The Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Amendment Bill, 2024, was recently tabled in the Rajya Sabha, marking a significant step in India's efforts to address water pollution. The bill includes provisions for stricter enforcement mechanisms, increased penalties for violations, and enhanced monitoring of water quality. It also introduces new measures to promote water conservation, sustainable usage, and the protection of water resources.
